## 2. Audio & Microphone Access

Nostos **does not record, collect, transmit, or store audio** from users.

The Android permission `android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO` is included automatically by the audio playback framework used in the app to support features such as:

* Media playback
* Audio focus handling
* System interruption management

The App **never activates the microphone** and **does not perform audio recording** at any time.
No voice data is collected or shared.

The app may also modify system audio settings (such as volume and audio focus) strictly to ensure proper media playback behavior.
